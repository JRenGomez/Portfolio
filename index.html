The Problem

Americans waste about 60 million tons of food a year. Restaurants are a big contributor to the problem. Wasted food adds to landfills which harms the environment

The disconnect between surplus food and budget friendly distribution, continues to contribute to the environmental destruction as well as economic pains.

User Research

as a team our Interview Objectives were to: Learn about the user’s day to day life and habits, Understand the user’s wants and needs, and Get an idea of the relationship between the user and the problem. While beginning our investigation, we started to think about the people that might need food security. We tried to think of the people most affected by food waste in a broad sense.
